Kawasaki Legends

It has been more than 60 years since Kawasaki
started full-scale production of motorcycles. Our
first motorcycle engine was designed based on
technical know-how garnered from the develop
-ment and production of aircraft engines. Our entry
into the motorcycle industry was driven by
Kawasaki's constant effort to develop new technolo
-gies. Over the years we have released numerous
new models that have helped shape the market,
and in the process created many enduring legends
based on the speed and power of our machines.
